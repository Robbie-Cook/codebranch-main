Our Fees...


Under 3 years old


For our under 3 children we have a Hourly Charge of $6.00 per hour.

Daily Charge of $39.00 per day

Weekly Charge of $190.00 per week.

​3 year olds and over
​
Hourly charge of $6.00 per hour

For 3 and 4 year olds we have 20ECE hours available. These are funded by the government and can be taken at the maximum of 6 hours per day and 20 hours total per week. These hours are FREE and we do not ask for any DONATIONS if these FREE hours are used like other centers.

Condtions of payment

​
Fees are to be paid two weeks in advance preferably by automatic payment

Bank Details: Yorkplace Limited 06-0939-0181801-00

​

All charges include GST

We accept WINZ subsidies

​

Sick days will be charged at normal rates. Statuatory holidasys and snow days will be charged. When the centre is closed during the Christmas/New Years period there will be no charge for the days we are closed.

A late charge of $5 for every 5 minutes after the booked in time, unless you have notified staff you are going to be late.

​

Holiday charges - we offer half price rates for up to 10 Days nabsent due to holidays per year. PLease notify us in writing 2 weeks in advance for these rates..

​

We require 2 weeks written notice of a child leaving the centre. If notice is not given then two weeks fees will be charged in lieu.
