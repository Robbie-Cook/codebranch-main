Welcome to York Place Preschool & Nursery!


Located in the heart of Dunedin, York Place Preschool & Nursery is the perfect early childcare centre for newborns up to 5 year olds. This preschool has been involved in Dunedin childrens education for more than 30 years and is continuing to achieve the highest level of care and education when caring for your children. We are very close to the City Centre, University of Otago and Octagon.

Open from 7:30am to 6:00pm Monday to Friday for your convenience. Our childcare centre offers a nurturing and educational environment for your children.

The center has great indoor outdoor flow. This is important to us to ensure your child receives a great mix of class room time and outdoor activites.

Childcare in the heart of Dunedin

With an extremely central location in the heart of Dunedin and only minutes to Geroge Street, the Octagon and the Universty of Otago, our  childcare centre is great for parents working in the city centre or University of Otago. This is particularly beneficial for breastfeeding mothers, who are invited to visit their babies during the day to breastfeed if their schedules allow.

Our Classes

Our childcare centre is spilt into a preschool and a nursery on the same site, so the children are being taught specifically to their age group

   - Nursery Ages 0 - 2
   -Preschool Ages 2 - 5  

We allow the children to have the choice of moving from the nursery to the preschool when they feel ready and once they have turned 2 years of age. This starts with visits with a nursery teacher to the preschool area. These become more frequent until the child is ready to move.
